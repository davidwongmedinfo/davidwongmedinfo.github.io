Hi there!

I am Wang Dawei (David) , currently a bioinformatics engineer, specializing in deep learning-assisted novel drug design.

Previously, I worked at the Cancer Immunology Lab at Peking University, where I was engaged in wet-lab and bioinformatics research. Under the joint supervision of Prof. Yanhui Yin and Prof. Yuan Zhou, I conducted a study that integrated bulk RNA-Seq and scRNA-Seq approaches to analyze the heterogeneity of the glioblastoma microenvironment. I received my bachelor's degree from Xiamen University in 2020.

I am passionate about physics, electronic engineering and computer science, and I have always striven to explore new domains, which I found truly thrilling and inspiring. My aspiration lies in developing and using cutting-edge computational methods to tackle biomedical problems, producing influential research, and with the ultimate goal of facilitating individualized precision medicine.

During my leisure time, I enjoy strolling and meditation.
